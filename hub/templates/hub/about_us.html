About EduCycle

Connecting students through sustainable commerce

Our Mission

To create a sustainable, student-driven marketplace that promotes community, reduces waste, and makes campus life more affordable.

EduCycle was born from a simple observation: students constantly need to buy and sell items, but existing platforms are either too complex, too expensive, or too impersonal. We wanted to create something that feels like it was built specifically for campus life.

Sustainable Commerce

Every transaction helps reduce waste and promote reuse on campus.
